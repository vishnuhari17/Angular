About Bhitarkanika

Bhitarkanika Mangrove Forest is a unique ecosystem located in the Kendrapara district of Odisha. It is home to a variety of flora and fauna, including saltwater crocodiles, exotic birds, and lush mangroves. The forest is a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts.
